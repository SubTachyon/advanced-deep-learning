The results are honestly kind of unsatisfying though. The model seems to converge pretty early, but the samples still look messy and not that convincing. At this point I am mostly phoning this one in. I got the model training, made the plots, and the output is not complete nonsense, but I am tired and did not manage to turn it into something I am actually happy with.
